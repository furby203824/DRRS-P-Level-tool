MO/ME derived from T/O PayGrade (O/W = Officer, E = Enlisted). NC/NE split requires Service column in roster (USN vs USMC). T/O Structure does not carry Service, so Navy billets show in the Officer/Enlisted totals.

UIC and Unit Name are read from the T/O Structure CSV when it loads. As-Of Date is operator-supplied and auto-saved to your browser. All three stamp onto the readiness brief, JSON export, and audit CSV filename.
